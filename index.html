With a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering and over four and a half years of professional experience, I offer a strong proficiency in a diverse range of technologies. My prior roles at Financials Unlimited and Codematics Services have further expanded my skill set, providing hands-on experience in Vue.js, Vuex, React.js, state management, Socket.io, WebRTC, Node.js, and Progressive Web Application (PWA) development.
